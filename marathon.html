18-Week Half Marathon Training Plan

Race Day: November 9 | Beginner Friendly

Week 1 (Jul 07 - Jul 13)

Mon: Strength - Full body (stability & mobility)

Tue: Easy Run - 2.0 miles

Wed: Cross-Training - Low impact

Thu: Speed - 4x1 min fast w/ 90 sec walk

Fri: Strength + Mobility - Glutes, core, stability

Sat: Long Run - 3 miles

Sun: Rest or active recovery

Mon: Strength - Full body

Tue: Easy Run - conversational pace

Sun: Rest or active recovery
